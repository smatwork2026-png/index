Rules of Cricket

  • Players: Har team mein 11 players hotay hain.
  • Umpire: Match ko chalane ke liye 2 on-field umpires hotay hain.
  • Scoring: Runs bananay ke liye batsman daudta hai ya boundary marta hai (4 ya 6 runs).
  • Out Hona: Batsman bowled, caught, lbw, runoff, ya stump out ho sakta hai.
